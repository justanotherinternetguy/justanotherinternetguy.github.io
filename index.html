    • justanotherinternetguy's plane of the week

    F-107 "Ultra Sabre"

    The North American F-107 "Ultra Sabre" - North American's entry for a new supersonic fighter-bomber aircraft. Capable of Mach 2+ and using new innovations such as the over-fuselage air intake and spoiler-induced roll at supersonic speeds, it unfortunately eventually lost out to the F-105. However, not everything on the F-107 went to waste. The spoiler-induced roll system can be found on the (very) successful B-1 Lancer bomber. The F-107 also pioneered the variable geomtetry air intake ramp, which was developed and used on the A-5 and XB-70. Some call it the "best fighter that never was."
