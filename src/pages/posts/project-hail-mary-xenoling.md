#

I recently watched [Project Hail Mary](<https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Project_Hail_Mary_(film)>) with a few friends. Having already read the book many years ago, I was highly anticipating the movie. It did not disappoint! However, this post does not intend to be a movie review. Rather, as I watched the movie, the whimsy-seeking linguistics nerd in my mind lit up as I watched Dr. Grace attempt to establish communication with Rocky. Ignoring the [Dark Forest Hypothesis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dark_forest_hypothesis) and other philosophical arguments for communication with aliens, I wondered what communication with aliens would really look like.

> I understand just how unrealistic (and possibly stupid) this scenario may sound, but humor me for a second...

For the purposes of this random investigation into linguistics, let's assume we come across a neutral alien spaceship that we intend to attract and establish communication with. Let's break down what Grace and Rocky do to communicate and what other possibilities could be out there.
