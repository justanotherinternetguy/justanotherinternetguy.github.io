# Project 1Week

I've always believed in the power of hyperobsession. I've had great success in learning and making headway in a subject when I spend every minute of my free time, every waking hour, consuming content about and exploring that topic.

I wanted to apply the same idea into learning skills and subjects by hyperobsessing over that subject for 1 week. I'll strive to spend the maximum amount of time physically possible learning about and practicing a chosen topic. Every moment I am not asleep or working on must-do commitments I will devote to that subject only. I'll do this for every week with a new topic. If a topic/project (e.g making my own OS) is too large of a project, I may split it into smaller goals over the course of several weeks.

I don't expect to master anything. The goal is to push myself to learn _how to learn_ topics as fast as possible and most efficiently while exploring all sorts of disciplines. I'll update my blog with progress and notes every few days to keep myself accountable. -->
